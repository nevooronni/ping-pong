PING PONG

Simple, Easy and Awesome to Play

Enter a number a a press get to play ping pong

RULES

Ping Pong will count up to your number starting from 1 with the following exceptions

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 become "ping"
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 become "pong"
  3. Numbers divisibel by 3 and 5 become "pingpong"
